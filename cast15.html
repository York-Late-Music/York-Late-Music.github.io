25 January 2023

Interview: David Lancaster

Dr David Lancaster talks to Hayley Jenkins about the intriguingly named February lunchtime recital Au Lapin Agile: Setting — A French café in Montmartre — and how his piece of the same name has evolved from a flute and piano piece to a flute, bass clarinet, and guitar trio. There are four world premieres and one UK premiere, performed by Music on the Edge, in this concert to transport you to a little bit of Paris.
